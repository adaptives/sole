This is an online, peer based, social learning community for everyone interested in learning Computer Science or related topics. We support multiple learning styles.You can select one of our DIY courses, and learn at your own pace with the help of a supportive community, or you can choose to participate in a peer enabled study group. You will be able to establish social credentials as you participate in the community by earning points and badges for asking and answering questions. You can also submit your response to specific skill related challenges. You will be awarded a badge for that skill when sufficient participants upvote your response. Our aim is to foster a peer based learning community where you can have fun while learning and also establish Internet credentials for your learning endevours.
